About This App

Welcome to our To-Do List App! This application is designed to help you efficiently manage your tasks and stay organized. Built using Flask, a lightweight and powerful web framework, our app allows you to easily add, update, and delete your to-dos.

Features:

  • Add Tasks: Quickly add new tasks to your to-do list.
  • Update Tasks: Edit and update existing tasks with ease.
  • Delete Tasks: Remove tasks that are no longer needed.

Stay productive and never miss a task with our user-friendly To-Do List App!
